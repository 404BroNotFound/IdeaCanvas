Privacy policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

What is stored

IdeaCanvas saves canvas content in your browser first. This may include text, drawings, pictures, titles, and workspace settings. If you create an account, your canvas data is also stored in the connected Supabase project so it can be opened on your other devices.

Account information

Supabase Authentication processes your email address, encrypted password credentials, session information, and confirmation or recovery emails. IdeaCanvas does not ask for or receive your Gmail password.

Sharing and tracking

IdeaCanvas does not include advertising or analytics. Data is shared only with services required to host the site, load fonts, authenticate accounts, and store signed-in users’ canvases.

Your choices

You can work without an account, export an editable JSON backup, delete individual canvases, clear browser storage, or permanently delete your cloud account from the account dialog.

Contact

For questions or deletion support, open an issue in the IdeaCanvas GitHub repository.
